Social Scope: Public Reaction Analysis

Objective:
To analyze public reaction and sentiment towards unique or humorous Instagram reels, and provide content creators with insights into how their audience is responding to their content.

Case Study 1: Sketching Random Strangers

Instagram Reel: This guy draws random people – what was the public reaction?

Objective: Analyze the audience's reaction to a humorous sketch of a random person in the video.

Analysis: Extract and analyze comments to determine the overall sentiment and level of engagement. Was the sketch perceived as funny? How did it resonate with the audience?

Case Study 2: Explaining Song Lyrics

Instagram Reel: Decoding "Charkhaa" lyrics – how did it go?

Objective: Examine the audience's understanding and appreciation of the reel where song lyrics are explained.

Analysis: Evaluate comments to assess how well the explanation was received. Was it informative? Did it enhance the viewer's understanding of the song?

Case Study 3: Temple Analysis

Instagram Reel: Exploring a historical temple – what insights were gained?

Objective: Analyze audience engagement with a reel showcasing the exploration of a historical temple.

Analysis: Determine the public's reaction to the content. Did viewers find it educational or inspiring? What kind of discussions or reflections did it generate in the comments?

Outcome:

These analyses will help content creators understand the impact of their posts and adapt their strategies to better engage with their audience. By capturing public sentiment, creators can refine their content to better meet the interests and expectations of their viewers.
